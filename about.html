My Story

My name is Amanda Mu’amoholeva, I graduated as a Nationally Certified Pharmacy Technician, and have been in the medical field for over 12 years, with this career I fully focused on patient care.

While I absolutely loved advocating for patients I quickly found myself getting frustrated with the Healthcare industry, once being all about patient centered care turned into a business making system. I wanted to take the skills I learned in the past 12 years and do something good with them. That's when I discovered UX design. The more I learned the more I fell in love, it was like UX design was made specifically for me! I would be able to show a passionate understanding with people who are trying to better themselves with their day to day lives and make products specifically made for them.

When I’m not chasing my dream as a UX Designer I’m either with my family enjoying finding the next outdoor activity or just being outside having a BBQ with family and friends. My husband and I are usually on one remodeling project or another around our home. I enjoy playing any type of game from board games to card games. You’ll also find me in the kitchen trying new recipes. A typical winding down activity that I enjoy would be watching a movie with my little family in the comfort of our home, or just grabbing a book and getting cozy on the couch.
